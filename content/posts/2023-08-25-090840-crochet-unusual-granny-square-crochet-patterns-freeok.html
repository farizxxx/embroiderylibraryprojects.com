What are Granny Squares?

If you're new to crochet, you might be wondering what a granny square is. A granny square is a type of crochet motif that is typically made with three or more rounds of crochet. The square can be made in any size, and it is usually used to create blankets and afghans. The traditional granny square is made up of clusters of double crochet stitches that create a three-dimensional texture.

Granny squares are versatile and can be used to create many different types of designs. They can be combined in different ways to make stripes, diagonals, and geometric patterns. You can also play with color to create a unique design for your blanket.
