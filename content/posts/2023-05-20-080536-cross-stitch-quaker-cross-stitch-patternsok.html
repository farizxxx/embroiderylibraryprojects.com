What are Quaker Cross Stitch Patterns?

Quaker Cross Stitch Patterns are a type of embroidery that originates from the Quaker community. They are characterized by their simplicity, with designs often featuring geometric shapes and clean lines. These patterns were historically used for samplers, which were pieces of embroidery used to teach young girls how to stitch. However, in recent years, Quaker Cross Stitch Patterns have gained popularity amongst both beginners and experienced embroiderers.
