What is a Cross Stitch Pattern?

A cross stitch pattern is a detailed chart that shows you how to create a specific image using embroidery thread and fabric. The chart will typically show you which color thread to use and where to stitch it on the fabric. Cross stitch patterns have been around for hundreds of years and have been used to create beautiful and intricate designs.
