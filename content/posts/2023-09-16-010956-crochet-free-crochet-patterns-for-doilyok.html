What are doilies, anyway?

A doily is a decorative piece of fabric or paper that is mainly used to protect surfaces from scratches or stains caused by vases, lamps, or other decorative objects. However, in the world of crochet, a doily refers to a circular or oval-shaped decorative piece made out of cotton thread or yarn.

Doilies come in various sizes and shapes, and can be used as a placemat, tablecloth, or as a standalone decorative item. They are also great as a gift, especially when accompanied by other crocheted items such as coasters, dishcloths, or pot holders.
