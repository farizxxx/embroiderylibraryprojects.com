Cross Stitching Basics

If you're new to cross-stitching, don't worry! It's a relatively easy craft to pick up. Here's a quick run-down of the basics:

Materials

To get started with cross-stitching, you'll need a few basic materials:

  • Cross stitch fabric (often Aida cloth)
  • Cross stitch floss (thread)
  • A needle
  • An embroidery hoop (optional but recommended)

Stitching

Once you have your materials, it's time to start stitching! The basic stitch in cross-stitching is called the "cross-stitch" (surprise!). It's made up of two diagonal stitches that cross over each other to form an X on the fabric.

To make a cross-stitch, follow these steps:

  1. Bring your needle up through the bottom left-hand corner of the square you want to stitch.
  2. Take your needle down through the top right-hand corner of the square.
  3. Bring your needle up through the bottom right-hand corner of the square.
  4. Take your needle down through the top left-hand corner of the square.
  5. You should now have a neat little X in your fabric!
