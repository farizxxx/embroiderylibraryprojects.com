The fabric and thread you choose will have a big impact on the final look of your cross stitch pattern. For Pokemon designs, aida fabric and DMC floss are great choices. Aida fabric has a grid-like texture that makes it easy to count stitches, while DMC floss is available in a wide range of colors, perfect for creating colorful Pokemon designs.

Take Your Time

Cross stitch patterns Pokemon can be complex and time-consuming to work on. It's important to take your time and work at a pace that feels comfortable for you. Rushing through your work can lead to mistakes or uneven stitching.

Use a Light Source

A light source can help make it easier to see your stitches and keep track of your pattern. A desk lamp or a daylight lamp can provide the right amount of light without straining your eyes.
