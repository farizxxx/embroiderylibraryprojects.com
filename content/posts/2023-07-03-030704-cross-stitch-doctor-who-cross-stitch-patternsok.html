What is Cross Stitching?

Cross-stitching is a popular hobby that has been around for centuries. It involves creating an image by stitching colored threads onto fabric that is marked with a specific pattern. The end result is a beautiful piece of art that has been created by the crafter's own two hands. Cross-stitching is an incredibly versatile hobby, as it can be used to create patterns for clothing, home decor, and even a standalone piece of artwork.
