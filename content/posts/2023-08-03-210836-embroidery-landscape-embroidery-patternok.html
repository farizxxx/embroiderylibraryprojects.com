What is Landscape Embroidery?

Embroidery has been a popular form of textile art for centuries. It involves creating designs on fabric using a variety of stitches and threads, often with intricate details and beautiful patterns. Landscape embroidery is a specific type of embroidery that focuses on capturing the essence of nature, from the rolling hills and fields to the serene calm of the ocean.

Landscape embroidery patterns are often used to create wall art or banners that can be hung in homes, offices, or other spaces. They can also be used to create clothing, bags, and other accessories.
