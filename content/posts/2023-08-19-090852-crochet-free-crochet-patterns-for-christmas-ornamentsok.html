Crochet Christmas Tree Ornament Pattern For Beginners

For those who are just starting with crochet, making a Christmas tree ornament can be a great way to get some practice. The pattern we found is easy to follow and produces beautiful results.

Pattern

The Crochet Christmas Tree Ornament requires only basic crochet skills and can be customized to suit your preferences. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start with a magic circle
  2. Chain 1
  3. Single crochet 10 stitches into the circle
  4. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet
  5. Chain 1
  6. Single crochet 2 stitches into each stitch around the circle (20 stitches total)
  7. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet
  8. Chain 1
  9. Single crochet into each stitch around the circle (20 stitches total)
  10. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet
  11. Chain 1
  12. Single crochet 2 stitches into the first stitch, then single crochet into the next stitch (30 stitches total)
  13. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet
  14. Chain 1
  15. Single crochet into each stitch around the circle (30 stitches total)
  16. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet and cut yarn
  17. Weave in the ends and add a ribbon or string to hang the ornament
