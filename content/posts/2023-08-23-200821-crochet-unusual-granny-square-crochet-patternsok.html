The Scramble Square Crochet Blanket Pattern

One of the most unusual granny square crochet patterns is the Scramble Square Crochet Blanket. This pattern doesn't follow the traditional granny square pattern of rounds being worked on top of each other. Instead, each round is worked in a different direction, creating a beautiful and unique mosaic effect. The pattern looks intricate, but it's actually quite easy to follow, making it a great project for beginners who want to try something different.
