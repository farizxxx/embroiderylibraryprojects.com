Horses are majestic creatures that have captured the hearts of many. It's no surprise that they are a popular choice for embroidery designs. Here are some stunning embroidery designs of horses that you can use for your projects.

PAINT HORSE EMBROIDERY DESIGN

This beautiful embroidery design showcases the beauty of a paint horse. The intricate details of the embroidery make it look like the horse is almost alive. Use this design on towels, tote bags or even as a framed piece for your home.

BROWN HORSE FREE EMBROIDERY DESIGN

This embroidery design features a realistic looking brown horse. The design is free and can be downloaded from the website. Use this design to create beautiful horse-themed projects like blankets or even a jacket for a horse rider.

WILD HORSES EMBROIDERY DESIGNS BY AMAZING DESIGNS!

This embroidery design features a herd of wild horses running free. The design comes from Amazing Designs and is perfect for creating stunning pieces like a throw pillow or even a wall hanging.

HORSE PHOTO STITCH FREE EMBROIDERY DESIGN 11

Get creative with this horse photo stitch embroidery design. The intricate details of the design make it look like a stunning black and white photograph. Use this design to create a one-of-a-kind horse-themed quilt or even as a focal point on a jacket.

CELTIC HORSES MACHINE EMBROIDERY DESIGNS

This embroidery design is perfect for those who love both horses and Celtic designs. The intricate details of the Celtic knots and the horses make for a stunning and unique design. Use this design to create a beautiful wall hanging or even on a tablecloth for a horse-themed dinner party.
