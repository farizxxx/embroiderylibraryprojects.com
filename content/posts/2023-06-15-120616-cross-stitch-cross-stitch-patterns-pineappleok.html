If you love cross stitching, then you might be interested in creating a pineapple design. Pineapples are not just delicious tropical fruits, they are also a popular motif in home decor and fashion. Here are some cross stitch patterns featuring pineapples that you can use as inspiration for your next project.

Free Cross Stitch Patterns : Pineapple Cross Stitch Pattern

This free pattern from blogger Renata's Designs has a charming naive style that would look great on kitchen towels or totes. The pattern features a yellow pineapple with green leaves, stitched on a white background. It uses only whole stitches and backstitching, making it ideal for beginners.

Geometric pineapple Cross Stitch pattern geometric cross

This pattern from Etsy shop PixlStitch is perfect if you want a modern and minimalistic pineapple design. It features a grey pineapple with a white background, stitched in a geometric style that resembles a cross. The pattern is available in multiple sizes and comes with both a chart and a color block key.

Pineapple cross stitch pattern Modern cross stitch Tropical

This pattern from Etsy shop BrookesBooksPublishing has a contemporary feel that would go well with a bohemian or tropical decor. It features a black pineapple with colorful flowers and leaves, stitched on a white linen. The pattern includes both a chart and a list of DMC floss colors.

Pineapple Cross Stitch Pattern by Susan Saltzgiver - Crosstitch.com

This pattern from Crosstitch.com is a classic design that would never go out of style. It features a yellow pineapple on a blue and white gingham background, stitched in a realistic style with shading and highlights. The pattern is available as a digital download and includes both a color chart and a black and white symbol chart.

Free Cross Stitch Pattern Pineapple | DIY 100 Ideas

This pattern from DIY 100 Ideas is another free one that has a playful and whimsical look. It features a red and green pineapple with text that says "Aloha" and confetti in the background. The pattern uses only whole stitches and some backstitching, and it comes with a list of floss colors.

Pineapple Cross Stitch Pattern Happy Fruit PDF Modern Cute

This pattern from Etsy shop TinyNeedleStitches has a cheerful and kawaii style that would appeal to kids and teens. It features a pink and green pineapple with a happy face and a strawberry on top, stitched on a white background. The pattern is available as a PDF file and includes a chart, a key, and tips for stitching on Aida fabric.

Cross Stitch Pattern Pineapple - Cross Stitch Patterns

This pattern from Etsy shop Mybobbin has a vintage and shabby chic feel that would fit in a farmhouse or cottage style decor. It features a beige pineapple with green and yellow leaves, stitched on a beige and white checkered background. The pattern is available as an instant download and includes a color chart, a DMC symbol key, and a list of stitches used.

Pineapple Counted Cross Stitch Pattern PDF Cross Stitch - Etsy

This pattern from Etsy shop HappySlothPatterns has a sophisticated and graphic style that would suit a modern and chic decor. It features a black and white pineapple with a bokeh effect, stitched on a grey background. The pattern is available as a PDF file and includes a chart, a legend, and a floss usage summary.

Pineapple Cross Stitch Pattern

This pattern from DMC has a classic and elegant look that would work well on a tablecloth or a cushion cover. It features a gold and green pineapple with black outlines, stitched on a cream and terracotta background. The pattern is available as a PDF download and includes detailed instructions, tips for fabric preparation, and a list of DMC threads.

Pineapple Counted Cross stitch Pattern PDF Cross Stitch | Etsy

This pattern from Etsy shop CrossStitchByLiza has a feminine and romantic style that would suit a bedroom or a dressing room. It features a pink and green pineapple with a floral wreath, stitched on a white linen. The pattern is available as a PDF file and includes a chart, a floss list, and instructions for stitching with two strands.
