Trick or Treat

As the name suggests, Trick or Treat is a cross stitch pattern that's perfect for Halloween. Designed by Ursula Michael, this pattern features a jack-o'-lantern, bats, and a spooky house. The pattern is stitched using a variety of colors, including black, purple, and orange. The finished design measures 9.5 inches by 5.5 inches on 14-count Aida fabric, and is sure to be a hit with anyone who loves all things Halloween.
