Cross Stitch Patterns: What Are They?

In case you are new to the world of cross stitching, let's quickly define what a cross stitch pattern is. At its most basic level, a cross stitch pattern is a set of instructions that tells you which colors to use and where to place each stitch in order to create a particular design. Cross stitch patterns can range from simple, beginner-friendly designs to complex and intricate works of art.
