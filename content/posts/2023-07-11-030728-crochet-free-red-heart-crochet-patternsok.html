What is Red Heart?

Red Heart is a well-known brand in the world of crochet and yarn. Founded in the 1930s, Red Heart has been providing quality products to crocheters for over 80 years. In addition to yarn, Red Heart offers a wide range of free patterns for individuals of all skill levels. Their website provides step-by-step instructions and illustrations for each pattern, making it easy to follow along.
