What is Just Another Button Company?

The Just Another Button Company is a cross stitch and craft company that offers beautiful and unique design patterns. The company was founded in 1998 by Diane Williams, and since then has been providing some of the most exquisite cross stitch patterns that are a joy to stitch. The company is located in Holland, MI, and not only offers cross stitch patterns but also specializes in handmade buttons that can be added to your cross stitch projects for an extra personal touch.

Why Choose Just Another Button Company Cross Stitch Patterns?

The Just Another Button Company cross stitch patterns are unique in their design as they include buttons to be added to your finishing touches. The company took cross stitching to the next level by offering patterns that contain beautiful buttons that are perfect for framing, ornaments, or even pouches. These buttons range from unique shapes, designs to sizes to choose from, which makes each pattern unique for your project.

The company also offers a range of patterns from patriotic, seasonal, and even a collection of stitches and buttons for a daily dose of inspiration. The patterns are also reasonably priced and offer a variety of difficulty levels for all levels of stitchers. Each pattern includes easy-to-read charts, clear instructions, and even free charts available on their website if you're looking to try a pattern before purchasing.
