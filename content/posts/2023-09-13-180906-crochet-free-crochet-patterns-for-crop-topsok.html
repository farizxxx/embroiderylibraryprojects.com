Choose a color and style that fit your personality. You can go for a bold color if you like to stand out or a subdued color if you like to keep it simple. Also, consider the style; choose a style that matches your overall appearance and taste.

How to Crochet a Crop Top

Here's how to crochet a simple crop top:

Materials:

  • 5.5mm Crochet Hook
  • Worsted weight yarn
  • Tapestry Needle

Instructions:

  1. Start by chaining 4. Slip stitch to the first chain to create a ring.
  2. Chain 3 (counts as first dc) and make 11 DC stitches into the ring.
  3. Join with a slip stitch to the top of the first stitch.
  4. Chain 3, then DC into same stitch as chain.
  5. Make 2 DC into each stitch around. Join with a slip stitch to the top of the chain 3.
  6. Repeat step 4 in the next round.
  7. Chain 3, then DC into same stitch as chain.
  8. Make 2 DC into each stitch around.
  9. Repeat step 4 in the next round, then chain 58 stitches and slip stitch it to the opposite side through the top stitch.
  10. Now, work back up to the top by repeating steps 4-9 until you reach the desired length.
  11. Tie off and weave in ends.
