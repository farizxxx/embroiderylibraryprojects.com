What is counted cross stitch?

Counted cross stitch is a form of embroidery that involves stitching a design onto a piece of fabric using a chart or pattern. The pattern is usually printed on a grid, and each square on the grid represents a stitch. The stitches are usually made with embroidery floss, which comes in a wide range of colors and textures.

Counted cross stitch is a popular hobby because it is easy to learn and is relatively inexpensive. It is also a great way to make personalized gifts for friends and family members.
